Everything you need 🚀

7 powerful screens, zero backend, all in one file.

💳
Smart Budget Tracking
Set a monthly limit. Live progress bar turns amber at 80% and red when you exceed it. Instant warnings.
📲
Parent Alert System
Exceed your limit twice in 3 months? Budget Buddy auto-generates a WhatsApp message to your parents with your full expense list.
🤝
Loan Tracker
Log money given to friends. Track pending vs returned amounts. One tap to mark as returned.
📊
Analytics
6-month bar chart, category donut chart, biggest expense, avg spend, active days. Know your habits.
🔐
Savings Locker
Auto-deducts your savings on the 2nd of every month. Password-protected vault. Full deposit history.
📅
Monthly History
Browse every past month. Expandable cards with mood indicators and a progress bar vs your limit.
